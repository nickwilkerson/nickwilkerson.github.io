Hi, I'm Nicholas Wilkerson!

My name is Nick Wilkerson, I am a fullstack software engineer! It is my background which has shaped me into the hard working, driven software developer I am today. My goal in life has been to find a career that I can be passionate about and proud of, which I believe is what I have found in coding.
